Understanding AQI Levels

0-50 Good

Excellent Air Quality

Air quality is satisfactory, and air pollution poses little or no risk to health.

Activities: All outdoor activities are safe for everyone.
51-100 Moderate

Acceptable Air Quality

Air quality is acceptable for most people. However, sensitive individuals may experience minor respiratory symptoms.

Activities: Unusually sensitive people should consider reducing prolonged outdoor exertion.
101-150 Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups

Health Concerns

Members of sensitive groups may experience health effects. The general public is less likely to be affected.

Activities: Sensitive individuals should limit prolonged outdoor activities.
151-200 Unhealthy

Everyone Affected

Some members of the general public may experience health effects; sensitive groups may experience more serious effects.

Activities: Everyone should limit prolonged outdoor exertion.
201-300 Very Unhealthy

Serious Health Effects

Health alert: The risk of health effects is increased for everyone.

Activities: Everyone should avoid prolonged outdoor activities.
301+ Hazardous

Emergency Conditions

Health warning of emergency conditions: everyone is more likely to be affected by serious health effects.

Activities: Everyone should avoid all outdoor activities.
